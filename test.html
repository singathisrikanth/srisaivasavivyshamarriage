About Us

Sri Sai Vasavi Vysha Marriage Bureau is a trusted matrimonial service dedicated to helping Telugu families find the right life partner. Based in Warangal, Telangana, we specialize in traditional and value-based matchmaking.

We believe marriage is not just about profiles, but about compatibility, family values, and long-term happiness. Every profile is personally verified and matches are suggested based on education, profession, horoscope, lifestyle, and family background.

Our mission is to provide a transparent, respectful, and reliable matchmaking experience that leads to happy and successful marriages.
